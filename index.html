About

We at the Rotaract Club of SRM Kattankulkathur are an 800-member strong team working from our base station in Chennai. As a part of District 3232 we function under the Rotary Club of Meenambakkam(Chennai) as our parent club.

We try to play problem-solvers who see a world where people unite and take action to create lasting change – across the globe, in our communities, and in ourselves-Being one of the first clubs to adopt The UNITED NATIONS' sustainable development goals as part of our mission.

Our members believe that we have a shared responsibility to take action on our world’s most persistent issues. Our members work together to:

  • Promote peace
  • Fight disease
  • Provide clean water, sanitation and hygiene
  • Save mothers and children
  • Grow local economies
  • Support education
